Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice

Discription

Chicken biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with tender chicken and aromatic spices. It is traditionally cooked using the "dum" method, enhancing its taste and aroma. With various regional variations, biryani is a popular festive and celebratory dish enjoyed globally. The combination of rice, chicken, and spices creates a satisfying and nourishing meal, making it a favorite among food enthusiasts worldwide.

Handi Chicken

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice

Discription

Handi chicken is a flavorful Indian dish made with tender chicken and a blend of aromatic spices. It gets its name from the traditional clay pot called "handi" in which it is cooked, enhancing the flavors. Handi chicken comes in various regional variations with unique spice combinations. It is a popular and cherished dish, often served during celebrations and special occasions. The succulent chicken and rich spices create a mouthwatering and satisfying meal that is loved by many across the globe

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Paneer
  • Veggies

Discription

Paneer tikka is a delicious Indian appetizer made with cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a flavorful mixture of spices and yogurt. The marinated paneer is then skewered and traditionally cooked in a tandoor (clay oven), imparting a smoky and charred flavor. It is also popularly prepared on a grill or in the oven. The combination of aromatic spices and creamy paneer creates a delectable taste that appeals to vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.
